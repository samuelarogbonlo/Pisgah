How it works
Four steps. Fully verified.
From the moment a doctor creates an order to the moment a patient receives their medication, every step is recorded on-chain.
01

Order created

Doctor creates a diagnostic order. Patient claims via World App with World ID verification.

02

Lab processes

Lab receives the order, collects sample, and uploads results. Result attested on-chain via EAS.

03

AI-assisted review

Claude analyzes results and drafts clinical summary. Doctor reviews, approves, and prescribes.

04

Verified delivery

Pharmacy prepares medication. Rider delivers with code verification. Delivery attested on-chain.
